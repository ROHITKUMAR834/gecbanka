Government Engineering College, Banka is a Government Engineering College working under the Department of Science and Technology. It has been established by the Government of Bihar in 2018. It is situated on the holy land where Satish Prasad Jha, one of seven martyrs who hoisted the flag of India at the Secretariat building near Patna on 11 August 1942. It belongs to the land of holy hill name The Mandar Hill.
